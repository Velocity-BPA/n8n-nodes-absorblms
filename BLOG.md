# Automate Your Learning Management with Absorb LMS for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node: n8n-nodes-absorblms, a comprehensive integration that brings the power of Absorb LMS directly into your n8n workflows.

## The Challenge of LMS Automation

Learning Management Systems are essential for modern organizations, but managing users, enrollments, courses, and certificates often involves tedious manual work or complex custom integrations. Whether you're onboarding new employees, tracking course completions, or generating compliance reports, these repetitive tasks drain valuable time from your L&D team.

## Introducing Absorb LMS for n8n

Velocity BPA has developed a powerful n8n community node that seamlessly connects your automation workflows with Absorb LMS. Built on the Absorb Integration API v2, this node empowers you to automate every aspect of your learning management processes without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features

Our Absorb LMS node provides comprehensive functionality across all major LMS operations:

**User Management**: Automatically create, update, and manage learner accounts based on triggers from your HR systems, CRM, or other data sources.

**Course & Enrollment Management**: Streamline course assignments by automatically enrolling users based on department, role, or custom criteria.

**Departments & Organization**: Maintain organizational structure by syncing department information across your tech stack.

**Certificates & Compliance**: Generate certificates automatically and track compliance status across your organization.

**Lessons & Content**: Manage learning content programmatically to keep your courses up-to-date.

**Analytics & Reporting**: Pull learning analytics into your reporting dashboards or trigger actions based on course completion and performance metrics.

## Getting Started

Installing the Absorb LMS node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-absorblms


After installation, restart n8n and you'll find the Absorb LMS node available in your node palette. Configure your API credentials and you're ready to start automating.
